Head Coach

Kyle Shanahan is an American football coach currently serving as the head coach of the San Francisco 49ers in the National Football League (NFL). He has worked as an offensive coordinator and assistant coach for multiple NFL teams including the Houston Texans, Washington Redskins, Cleveland Browns, Atlanta Falcons, and the San Francisco 49ers. Shanahan is known for his expertise in offensive play-calling, game planning, and developing top quarterbacks like Matt Ryan and Jimmy Garoppolo. His innovative and creative offensive schemes often involve using pre-snap motion and misdirection to confuse opposing defenses.
