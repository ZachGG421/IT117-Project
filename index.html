Recent News

49ers Sign Veteran WR Chris Conley

According to Ian Rapoport of the NFL Network, the San Francisco 49ers have signed Chris Conley to a one-year contract. Conley was originally drafted by the Kansas City Chiefs in the third round of the 2015 NFL Draft and spent four years with the team before moving on to the Jacksonville Jaguars. After two years in Jacksonville and a brief stint with the Houston Texans, Conley played seven games with the Tennessee Titans in 2022 before joining the 49ers.

Brock Purdy Successful UCL Surgery

According to the team announcement, 49ers Quarterback Brock Purdy has had a successful surgery on his elbow last Friday. Dr. Keith Meister, Purdy's orthopedic surgeon, performed an internal brace repair on his right elbow. The 49ers have stated that Purdy is expected to begin a throwing progression program in three months.

49ers Sign Jauan Jennings to 1-year tender

According to a report from Matt Barrows of The Athletic, Jauan Jennings, a 25-year-old wide receiver, has signed his one-year tender with the San Francisco 49ers. The team tendered a one-year contract to Jennings, who was an exclusive rights free agent in February. With the signing, Jennings will earn a salary of $940,000 and will become a restricted free agent next year.
